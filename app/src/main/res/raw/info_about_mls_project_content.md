BeaconDB is an a public database that collects location information about common wireless infrastructure (cell towers, WiFi access points, Bluetooth beacons). The cell tower data you contribute will be aggregated and released to the public under the "Creative Commons (CC-0)" license.

Tower Collector gives you opportunity to contribute in BeaconDB project by uploading cell towers from your area. [More information.](https://beacondb.net/)
